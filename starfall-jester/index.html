Controls

  • Move: WASD or arrow keys
  • Task: F or the task button
  • Report: R or the report button
  • Emergency: Q or the emergency button in Cafeteria, with a 5-minute shared cooldown
  • Kill: E or the kill button if you are the imposter
  • Revive: G if you are the reviver and are standing by a body
  • Act Suspicious: T if you are the jester
